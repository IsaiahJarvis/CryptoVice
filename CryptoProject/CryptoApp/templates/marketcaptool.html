Average Buy Order / Average Sell Order (in $) – Shows how much, on average, traders are spending per buy or sell in USD. A lower average order suggest smaller buyers are driving the market and a higher average spend suggests whales are getting involved.

Unique Buyer Ratio - Compares the number of unique wallets buying vs unique wallets selling. Above 1 and more unique wallets are buying, which indicates potential demand and interest. Below 1 and more unique wallets are selling, which indicates the development of bearish sentiment.

Buyer Retention Rate – Shows what % of buyers make repeat transactions, a higher % suggests higher holder confidence and long-term interest, which can help price remain stable.

Net Buys vs Net Sells – Shows if buyers or sellers are dominating transaction volume. Above 50% buyers are in control, below 50% sellers are more active.

Buy/Sell Ratio - Shows the relative market activity between buying and selling. If the ratio is above 50%, buying activity is stronger than selling, indicating buyers are in control. If the ratio is below 50%, selling activity is stronger than buying, suggesting sellers are in control.
